Use this simple form to classify which Iris-flower you found.

Viness is based on the Iris-flower dataset. I made a machine learning model based on the DecisionTreeClassifier. By manipulating the test/train-split to a 90%-training and 10% testing split the model came out with a 100% accuracy. This is because of overfitting, what this essentially means is that while my model has a 100% accuracy on the dataset it might not have 100% accuracy on your dataset. That explains why mishaps might happen.
